Dimensional Model (star schema):

Dimensions - aka Look up tables - (The points on the star)

Fact table - Middle of the star


Dimensions (example):

What: Product (what was sold)

When: Date (when it was sold)

Who: Reseller (who sold it)

Who: employer (who is assoicated)

Where: (Sales) Territory


Fact table: Stores transactions. Events that happened along the time


Note: we have to denormalize our database in order to find the right balance and build dimensions
